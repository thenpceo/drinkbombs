Our Story

Born From a
Pop Rock Moment

A mother, a hurricane, and a really good idea. Here's how Drink Bombs went from a kitchen experiment to 50+ flavors and counting.

The Founder

Chloe Di Leo

Chloe Di Leo didn't set out to reinvent the cocktail. She was trying to keep her kids hydrated.

It was Hurricane Harvey. Her daughter had run out of bath bombs. Her son came downstairs eating Pop Rocks. In that moment, the idea hit.

"I'm going to make you a fizzy drink bomb."
— Chloe Di Leo, Founder

She called a chemist. Together they dehydrated the full flavor profile of a cocktail — citrus, bitters, natural sweeteners, edible flowers — into a single dissolving sphere. Zero artificial anything.

What started as a hydration treat for kids became something much bigger. Today, Drink Bombs comes in 50+ flavors and counting.
