Our Story

Born From a
Boring Drink

It started at a party. The kind where someone spends $80 on cocktail ingredients, uses each bottle once, and still ends up making something that tastes like an apology.

We watched a friend spend 20 minutes muddling, shaking, and measuring — just to produce a single mediocre Mojito. Meanwhile, the sparkling water on the counter sat there, simple and perfect, doing absolutely nothing.

"What if you could drop something into that glass and it just... became a cocktail?"

That question became an obsession. We spent months engineering the perfect dissolvable sphere — getting the acid balance right, the effervescence dramatic, the flavor authentic. Not a powder. Not a liquid. A ritual object that transforms your glass into theater.

Today, every Drink Bomb is made with natural flavors, clean sweeteners, and zero artificial colors. Each one makes 2 drinks and works as a cocktail or mocktail. Because the best drink at the party shouldn't require a bartending degree.
